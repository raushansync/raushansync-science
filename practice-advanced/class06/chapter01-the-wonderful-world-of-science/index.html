9. The ‚ÄúInfinite Puzzle‚Äù of Science

Sample Answer:
A normal jigsaw puzzle has a fixed number of pieces and one final picture. Science is different because it has no final picture‚Äîevery new discovery leads to more questions. That is why science is described as unending.

10. Analyzing Variables (Pen Scenario)

Sample Answer:
If multiple things are changed at once (for example, changing the refill and paper together), we cannot know which change caused the result. Science requires testing one factor at a time to reach correct conclusions.
