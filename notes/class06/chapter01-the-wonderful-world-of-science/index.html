1. What Science Really Is

Science is much more than just facts in a book. It is a structured way to study and understand the natural world.

  • Seeing and Noticing: Observing patterns in nature.
  • Thinking and Asking: Questioning why and how.
  • Power of Curiosity: Drives discovery.
The Puzzle Idea: Science is like a growing puzzle. Wrong ideas are corrected as knowledge improves.
